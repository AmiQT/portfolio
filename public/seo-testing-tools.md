## Quick SEO Checklist:
- ✅ Meta titles and descriptions
- ✅ Header tags (H1, H2, H3)
- ✅ Image alt tags
- ✅ Internal linking
- ✅ Mobile responsiveness
- ✅ Page loading speed
- ✅ SSL certificate (HTTPS)
- ✅ Sitemap and robots.txt
